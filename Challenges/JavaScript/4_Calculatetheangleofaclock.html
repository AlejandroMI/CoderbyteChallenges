Calculcate the angle on a clock

This problem asks you to determine the angle between the to hands on a clock. For example, if the minute hand is on the 3 and the hour hand is on the 12, then this forms a 90 degree angle. If the hour hand is slightly past the 2 and the minute hand is on the 4, then the angle formed between the hands is 50 degrees. In this case we are taken the current time.
